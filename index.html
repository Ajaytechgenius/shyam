How it's built

Craftsmanship, made visible.

Every vehicle moves through the same eight-stage process — no shortcuts, regardless of the model on the lift.

01

Vehicle inspection

Full condition assessment before any work begins.

02

Decontamination

Chemical and mechanical decontamination of the paint surface.

03

Paint correction

Swirls and oxidation removed to restore true clarity.

04

Design & pattern prep

Wrap patterns and panel-specific film cut digitally for precision fit.

05

Installation

Wet installation and mod fitment in a climate-controlled bay.

06

Edge & finish work

Edges wrapped and trimmed for a factory-clean line.

07

Quality inspection

Panel-by-panel review under controlled lighting.

08

Delivery and aftercare

Care guidance and warranty registration on handover.
