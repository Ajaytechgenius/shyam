How it's built

Craftsmanship, made visible.

1. Inspect
2. Prep
3. Install
4. Delivery

Vehicle Inspection

Full condition assessment before any work begins. We measure paint depth, check for prior repairs, and map out a precise strategy tailored to your vehicle.

Decontamination & Prep

Chemical and mechanical decontamination of the paint surface. Swirls and oxidation are carefully removed via multi-stage correction to restore true factory clarity.

Precision Installation

Wet installation and mod fitment executed in our climate-controlled bay. Patterns are cut digitally, and edges are wrapped and trimmed for a seamless, invisible line.

Quality Check & Delivery

A rigorous panel-by-panel review under specialized studio lighting. Your handover includes complete care guidance and formal warranty registration.
